The Best TV Series According to Payal

My top 4 TV series of all-time.

1. F.R.I.E.N.D.S

This is my first ever sitcom. The unique characters, the story that unfolds between them & the amazing humour make me love it a lot. Ive re-watched the entire series atleast 10 times till now & it will be my go-to show to watch during mhy meals. Also. its the first show where me and my husband now bonded over when we were friends.

2. Breaking Bad

This is the second favourite show for both of us. My husband watch it with me the second time. Sometimes, it felt that the story went slow, but the amazing story line, mind blowing characters and the end, no show can beat it in thrillers.
The character Walter White's reason to get involved into the drug manufacturing business that he mentions in the end is that he did it all for fun. He liked the feeling, even though it was illegal and constantly mentioned all through the show that he did it for his family. This actual reason somewhere is relatable for any illegal or wrong thing we do in life. We do it because we like it, we might not want to admit it, but it is the subconscious reason. And that was my favourite part of the show.

3. Big Bang Theory

BBT. The most nerdy show that ever exists. Loved everything, the characters, the science and my second favourite character, Sheldon. Just Muahh! A must watch for all scienece enthus.

4. Suits

When you are in love with formals,please watch this show! LOL! that's the reason I actually started watching it for the first time. The amazing attire of US lawyers, their attitude, their love for their work, amazing dialogues and beautiful songs, have added a lot of them to my favourites. Another must watch if you love seeing people solving problems!
